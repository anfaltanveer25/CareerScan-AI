# CareerScan AI

CareerScan AI is an Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) based career intelligence system designed to predict the impact of Artificial Intelligence adoption on employee job status. The system classifies jobs into three categories: **Modified**, **Replaced**, or **Unchanged**, helping individuals and organizations understand potential workforce changes caused by automation and AI technologies.

The project utilizes machine learning algorithms including Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and XGBoost to analyze workforce-related factors such as Automation Risk, AI Adoption Level, Education, Experience, and Productivity Changes. To ensure transparency and trust, the system integrates SHAP and LIME explainability techniques that provide both global and local explanations for model predictions.

In addition to predictive analytics, CareerScan AI incorporates a Large Language Model (LLM) powered by Groq's Llama 3.3-70B to generate personalized career recommendations based on prediction results and user profiles. A Flask-based web application enables real-time interaction through a user-friendly questionnaire interface, allowing users to receive instant predictions, explanations, and career guidance.

The goal of CareerScan AI is to bridge the gap between workforce analytics and practical career decision support by combining Machine Learning, Explainable AI, and Generative AI into a single intelligent platform.
